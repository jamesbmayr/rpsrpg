RPS RPG
Three fantasy heroes team up to fight through a randomly generated dungeon of orb-hoarding monsters.
Launch
Every game has a unique 4-letter id. Create a game on a computer.
Join on a mobile device by entering the 4-letter id.
Gameplay
Swarms of menacing monsters have stolen the sacred orbs!

BARBARIAN: use strength to obliterate scurrying spiders
WIZARD: summon spells to destroy raging rock monsters
RANGER: throw dangerous daggers through ghastly ghouls

Work together to recover the orbs!
